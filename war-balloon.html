War Balloons

Hot Air Balloons were used for strategical advantages during the civil war. This was because they could soar up to 1000 feet into the sky and provide a birds eye view on opposing forces. In a time before airplanes were invented, having the ability to go far into the sky provided major advantages against adversaries. While both sides made use of the War Balloon, the Union was more organized and successful in their efforts.
