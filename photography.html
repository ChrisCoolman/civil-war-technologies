Civil War Photography

The Civil War was the first war that had photography, which provided a modern source of documentation. The first camera was invented in 1816, so when the Civil War broke out in 1861, cameras were able to capture moments during the war. Matthew B. Brady is known for taking photos and hiring photographers during the Civil War. Below are images taken during the Civil War era in towns, battlefields, and even the seas.
